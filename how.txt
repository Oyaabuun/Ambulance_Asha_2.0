## Proposed Changes

### 1. Backend Enhancement [MODIFY] [backend/main.py](file:///d:/Hackathons/gemma_hackathon/backend/main.py)
- **Hybrid-Edge Logic**: 
  - `gemma4:e2b` (The Sprinter): Handles immediate voice-to-triage and tool calling (Hospital Alerts).
  - `gemma4:26b` (The Specialist): Triggered for "Deep Analysis" to provide complex medical protocols and risk assessments.

### 2. Frontend Revolution [NEW] [frontend/*](file:///d:/Hackathons/gemma_hackathon/frontend)
- **Framework**: Next.js 15+ (App Router).
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion for premium animations.
- **UI Components**:
  - **The Pulse Dashboard**: A dark-mode, glassmorphic interface with a pulsing "Live" indicator.
  - **Triage HUD**: Large, color-coded priority levels (RED/YELLOW/GREEN) that animate on change.
